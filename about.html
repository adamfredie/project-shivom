Our Vision

We enter the Age of Genomics

With currently 7.6 billion people on earth, every one of them has a unique code trapped within their genes that unlocks the secrets to their appearance, ancestry, intellectual capabilities, health and fitness. But, its ability to reveal their predisposition to disease has been overlooked. With DNA sequencing made economical, we are moving into a new era of genomics, reaching possibilities of analysing millions of genomes and presenting a new dawn in opening up access to personalized healthcare on a global scale.

The opportunity for global health

The next challenge in genomic science is collection of massive genomic data sets to make it useful and accessible. The few key players hold the monopoly and make huge profits from selling data to 3rd parties, often without permission from or incentivisation to the data donor. Other sources of genomic data are small, soloed, inaccessible, and hence are unable to reach their full potential to improve healthcare.

Genome-based precision medicine: the future is here now

At Shivom, we are changing the dynamics of the global healthcare market. We are utilizing the most advanced technologies including blockchain, cloud computing, genome sequencing, artificial intelligence and big data analytics to bring medical genomics into a new era.

Your data belongs to you!

Using cryptography and next-generation blockchain technology, people will be securely and anonymously able to share their data with a variety of stakeholders at will, allowing them to help drive healthcare and also remunerated/get paid in return. Similarly, we envision a service where pharma companies can access information datasets to better drive their drug development programs, clinical trial design, and precision medicine initiatives.

The Shivom Foundation

The extent to which our genomics ecosystem realizes its potential will depend in substantial part upon how well stakeholders steward its development. We believe that such an important global initiative for the greater good should be steered into the right direction by a private-sector, international consortium. As such we will form a not-for-profit, global foundation that will ensure security, sustainability and proper ethical conduct of the system.

The Shivom blockchain ecosystem must be distributed around the world and operated by trusted institutions in multiple countries, forming a decentralized network. Power will be distributed, a type of governance that is ideally suited to protect from government interference and subjugation.The foundations will strive to establish ethical and efficient stewardship, which involves collaborating, identifying common interests and creating incentives for all stakeholders to act on them.

Genomics for everyone

Accurate, comprehensive interpretation of your genome will only be possible when the biomedical community has analyzed enough genome sequences representing the full range of natural human variation. However, today, many ethnic and geographical populations are still underrepresented in public databases.

For people from those communities, genomic medicine is likely to be inaccurate because it is impossible to know which mutations are rare and potentially dangerous. Using a variety of sponsoring programs, our platform will change the status-quo, supporting projects to get people sequenced around the globe.

We are powering the next era of genomics creating a global personalized healthcare ecosystem to transform lives.
