The Shivom Ecosystem

Shivom is creating a medical genomics ecosystem on the blockchain and will offer an open web-marketplace for other healthcare providers to add their apps and services, alongside genomic data analytics and personalized medicine.

We are passionate about helping customers realizing their genomics challenge to develop safe, next-generation products and therapies. Shivom’s platform will provide valuable genetic information to be used by the world’s top pharmaceutical, diagnostic, and biotechnology companies, as well as leading academic, government, and nonpro

The platform works on principles of collaboration, openness, and integrity allowing patients to own their genomic data, have full access to it, and monetize it on the blockchain. To ensure widespread adoption, we offer a solution where everybody will be able to get their genome sequenced and stored. Each person’s genome can provide access to the latest health advances for many years to come.

Blockchain technology is ideally suited to address most trust issues, such as patient consent, unclear data ownership, data integrity, or user authentication, enabling complex data rights management and fine-grained access using smart contracts.

The integration of this blockchain platform with large-scale genomics, a health app marketplace, and global sponsoring programs will create seismic changes in how individuals receive medical care and how therapies will be developed.
